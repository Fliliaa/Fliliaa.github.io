All the time people are interested in learning new things. One of the most popular things is learning foreign languages. To start with, we will consider some benefits of learning foreign languages.

Benefits of learning a foreign language

1. Prospects in professional sphere. If you can speak different language fluently, you get opportunity of working abroad. The technical progress has made the world much more united that means people move to different countries.

2. Travel opportunities. Knowing non-native language will unable you to travel, meet new people and get on well with them.

3. Improve thinking skills. If an individual speaks a language from another language family, it might increase their ability to think in abstract concepts. We develop key learning skills such as cognitive thinking and problem-solving.
4. Improve your memory. A lot of studies have shown that bilingual children perform better at tasks involving memorization and have a better working memory â€“ the kind of memory that allows us to store and process information short-term, usually while performing a task.
5. Improvement in creativity. It involves such skills as memory, perception, multitasking, decision-making, all of which are greatly improved by learning a second language. Being bilingual also improves your innovative thinking: partly, simply through exposing you to new vocabulary, concepts, and a whole new culture.
